MENTAL HEALTH

refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It involves the ability to manage stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Mental health is a crucial component of overall health, and it affects how people think, feel, and act. Factors such as biological, genetic, environmental, and lifestyle elements contribute to mental health.
