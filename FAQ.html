Frequently Ask Question (FAQ)

Q. What is mental health?
+
A.

Mental health refers to emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how individuals think, feel, and act.

Q. What are common mental health disorders?
+
A.

Common disorders include depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and eating disorders.

Q. How can I maintain good mental health?
+
A.

Prioritize self-care, stay connected with others, manage stress, exercise regularly, and seek professional help when needed.

Q. What are the signs of depression?
+
A.

Persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, changes in sleep or appetite, fatigue, and feelings of worthlessness.

Q. How can I help someone with a mental health issue?
+
A.

Offer support, listen without judgment, encourage professional help, and educate yourself about their condition.
